The Organizing Idea
Problems Compound
An organization that can't align can't build shared intelligence. Without intelligence it reinvents what it already knows. Reinvention without adoption leaves systems on the shelf. The six problems are sequential — and all active simultaneously.
How To Use This
Find Your Problem
Filter by your audience above. Read the sub-problems inside each problem. When one sounds like your organization, open it. Heart the modules that match your situation. Your selections build a live engagement in real time.
The Model
Modules Are Modular
Each module stands alone and delivers a concrete outcome. Most engagements combine 4–8 modules across two or three problems. Some sequence naturally — none require each other. TCD helps configure the right set.
